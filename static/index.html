Laniakea Beach House

Fantastic Beachfront property with Newer Main House & additional Guest Cottage for sale or rent!

5 bedroom • 4 bath • 10 Sleeps • 2,827 sqft
Live the ultimate North Shore Life on Laniakea Beach, a renowned surf break, one of the best areas for wing foiling and kite surfing, and the most wonderful, prevailing light offshore winds. Centrally located with easy access to all of Oahu, while having miles of sandy beach out the back door.
The views from the deck are unparalleled. You have a perfect view of the surfers at both Himalayas (big wave like Jaws) and Laniakea, one of the best right handers in the world. You can also see the breaks of Hulton's, Jocko's, Chun's, and Pidleys.
‍
Discover this rare opportunity to own or stay at this wonderful oceanfront 5 bedroom, 4 bath home retreat nestled on the legendary North Shore of Oahu, also known as the Seven Mile Miracle, a stretch of coastline that boasts 36 world-class surf spots.

This spacious home has stunning ocean views and is located right on Laniakea Beach, fondly referred to as "Turtle Beach," due to the frequent visits by Hawaiian green sea turtles, or honu.
You’ll love luxuriating on your large lanai overlooking the surf, where you can also unwind in the evenings with breathtaking year-round sunsets and some delectable barbecue grilling.

Also, don’t miss out the miles of beach walking in the golden sand just outside your door.

One of the absolute best things to do is to enjoy the gorgeous 1.5 mile beach stroll from this Laniakea home all the way down to the charming town of Hale’iwa – it’s the vibrant heart of the North Shore with a good array of restaurants, surf shops, galleries, boutique shops, food trucks and its special own "historic" old plantation town feel.
Sometimes you can see turtles swimming a few feet away!

The Seasons

In the winter months, spot whales breaching out front and admire the beautiful surf that draws surfers from around the world to this region to experience its powerful waves and the chance to ride some of the most celebrated surf spots on Earth.

Outside of winter, the North Shore transforms into a tranquil escape with warmer, calmer conditions for swimming, tide pooling and snorkeling.

A Home Like No Other

This 61-651 Kamehameha Highway home is more than just a property; it’s an invitation to experience the best of Oahu’s North Shore. Whether you’re seeking a dream vacation rental or a permanent slice of paradise, this Laniakea Beach home is your gateway to the laid-back luxury, thrilling adventure, and authentic Hawaiian charm that make this area truly magical.
